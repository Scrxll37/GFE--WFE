ABOUT US

About Winning Fluid Equipment

Wenzhou Winning Fluid Equipment Co., LTD (WFE) specialises in the design and manufacture of industrial valves for more than 30 years. Our brands include WFE and GFE (Growth Fluid Equipment Co.,LTD), creating a reputation of quality and economical products in global market. GFE is a wholly owned subsidiary of WFE, whose main products are high quality ball valves.
